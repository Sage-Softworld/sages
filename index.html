about us

This Foundation was born out of a burning desire by our founders to give individuals who have been deprived and relegated the opportunity or chance at building and living the life they desire,
They are to be given a chance to live the life they desire, just as every other individual. Greens Foundation has gone far as to provide education, health and infrastructural aid to societies around every corner of the earth.
